Facts about dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. It has been proven that dogs can distinguish between different time intervals and even anticipate events based on temporal cues.

Dogs also know the difference between an hour and five. If conditioned, they can differentiate these time periods.

Did you know?

The Basenji dog is known as the "barkless dog" because it produces a unique yodel-like sound instead of a typical bark.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that dogs who were away from their owners showed the longest and the greatest amount of affection.
