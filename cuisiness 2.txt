<h1>Indian Food</h1>

<p>
India has many types of food. Different parts of India have different styles of cooking.
</p>

<h2>Types of Indian Food</h2>

<h3>North India</h3>
<p>Food made with wheat and gravy.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Butter Chicken</li>
  <li>Dal</li>
  <li>Naan</li>
</ul>

<h3>South India</h3>
<p>Mainly rice-based food.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Dosa</li>
  <li>Idli</li>
  <li>Sambhar</li>
</ul>

<h3>East India</h3>
<p>Simple food, often with fish.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Fish Curry</li>
  <li>Rasgulla</li>
</ul>

<h3>West India</h3>
<p>Mix of sweet and spicy food.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Dhokla</li>
  <li>Pav Bhaji</li>
</ul>

<h2>Spices</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Turmeric</li>
  <li>Cumin</li>
  <li>Chili</li>
</ul>

<p>
Indian food is an important part of Indian culture.
</p>
